About Me

Hey, I'm Justin! I'm currently a junior UC Berkeley student studying Electrical Engineering and Computer Science, and I'm also pursuing a minor in Bioengineering. Interests

Urban Dance

I first joined AFX, the largest urban dance club at Cal, during the fall semester of my sophomore year. I was initially hesitant to try out because I'd never danced ever before, and I was intimidated by the performances I'd seen at the showcase the year before. However, a few practices in, I realized that I really enjoyed the choreography and even more so, my teammates and directors. AFX has allowed me to relieve stress through dance, to meet new friends, and to express myself creatively. I've been with the club for three semesters now, and I honestly can't imagine another semester at Cal without dancing.
